Help us !

If you wish to support our cause, several choices are available to you :

  • You can engage as a volunteer (raising awareness in the street, giving lessons, helping with the psychological follow-up of victims, etc.)
  • You can talk about the association by sharing our social networks!
  • Finally, you can help financially, this financial assistance allows us to have access to premises where we can welcome the victims in all kindness, to finance awareness campaigns (printing of posters, stickers). You can either help us without counterpart, or buy our posters or our stickers to spread the word !
