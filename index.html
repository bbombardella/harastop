Online course

We offer you online conferences to explain what street harassment is, how to defend yourself against it, etc.

Meeting

We want to create moments of safe encounters for all victims of harassment so that you can discuss your suffering.

Inventory of safe places

We offer you a directory of places to know to be safe. These places have codes in place to pass on to them if you feel in danger.

Awareness in street

Thanks to our posters and our mobilizations, we make sure to raise awareness at the heart of the problem: in street.

Awareness online

Whether it is here on our website or on our social networks, we make sure to provide all the information necessary to raise awareness.

Raising Fund

In order to be able to help victims of harassment and welcome them to our premises, we have launched a fundraising campaign.
